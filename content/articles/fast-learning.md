## zero sum game

Something i started learning early is that life, is zero sum, just because someone else wins doesnt mean you nessecarily lost, and just because someone is winning that doesnt mean you have to be bitter. At my lowest, 5 years ago, I was bitter watching other people be in places i knew i could be but for reasons i will not disclose, wasnt. It took time to realize that if my friend wins and i dont, it doesnt mean that i lose, it means that they won and thats great for them. If anything, something ive learned especially in college, is that if my friends, i also win in some way. there is a benefit from other people winning in my life winning, it either boosts morale for me to win aswell, or gives me some benefit, (carlos and his codex). Something i live by and something i have been really good at in the past year is not seeing competition as me vs someone else, its me vs myself and then me vs world.
